The South Church Library isn't just a room. We've got books too! Now you can check out our entire collection of books from wherever you are. You can even check them out and take them home for a while! Feel free to stop in and say hello. The Library is run and managed by Carol-Georgine West. Questions about the library can be directed here. Technical questions about the website should be directed here.


This website was created by Justin and Lucas Bacchi in 2021 as part of Lucas's Eagle Scout Service Project. Lucas, along with family, friends, and other members of Troop 76 in Ballardvale, created this website and the database of books that powers it. The database was created with help from volenteers including but not limited to: Lucas Bacchi, Carol-Georgine West, Anna Bacchi, Justin Bacchi, Karen Bacchi, Doug Bacchi, Bill Mance, Owen Finlay, Greg Finlay, Dylan Hilse, Don Milligan, Lucy Henderson, Sashwat Patcha, Julia Kole, Despina Tasiopoulos, Kathryn Gatewood, Sriniketh Velagapudi, Kyle Huang, Craig Milligan, Sreekanth Patcha, Nagajyothi Patcha, Dan Bettencourt, Amy Bettencourt, Srinikhil Velagapudi, Dan Strellis, Owen Strellis, Scott Strellis, Kristin Strellis, Grady Lilley, Grace Lilley, and Julia Benjamin.
Thank you to all who volunteered! The project ran for 6 days beginning on Saturday, August 28th and ending on Sunday, September 5th. When all was said and done, the project totaled over 465 service hours.


Library Mission Statement: "The Library shall serve as a resource center of print and non-print materials to help people grow in their faith, to be a resource to the congregation in its various ministries, to enhance the educational ministry and support the Christian Education curriculum, to provide resources to help people in the issues and crises they face in everyday living, and to help people grow in their understanding of our church family."

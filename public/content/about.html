The South Church Library isn't just a room. We've got books too! Now you can check out our entire collection of books from wherever you are. You can even check them out and take them home for a while! Feel free to stop in and say hello (you know, once it's safe to do so).


This website was created by Lucas Bacchi in 2020 as part of Lucas's Eagle Scout Service Project. Lucas, along with family, friends, and other members of Troop 76 in Ballardvale, created this website and the database of books that powers it.
